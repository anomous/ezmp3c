Introduction
EZ MP3 Creator is a program used to extract digital music from audio cd and encode them into MP3, Ogg or WMA files. With the help of this program, you can store your cd music with high quality and small size. You can also extract cd tracks and save them as wave files without encoded.

EZ MP3 Creator uses these libraries, AKRip, GOGO and Ogg Vorbis. They are all open source freeware project.

AKRip is a small library designed to extract cd digital audio from audio cds. It can work with most ATAPI/IDE cdroms, and several SCSI/USB/FIREWIRE devices.

GOGO is a MP3 encoder based on LAME, which is a wonderful MP3 encode engine with perfect quality and high speed. It is optimized for Enhanced 3D Now!/SSE/SSE2.

Ogg Vorbis is a new audio compression format. It is roughly comparable to other formats used to store and play digital music. Even more it is a completely open, patent-free, professional audio encoding and streaming technology with all the benefits of open source.

EZ MP3 Creator supports multi cdroms.

EZ MP3 Creator has the ability of querying cd information such as album, artist, track titles from freedb servers over internet. The information is stored in MP3 ID3 tags, Ogg comments and WMA attributes.

EZ MP3 Creator is very easy to use with a wizard, you need only a few clicks to create your music files.
