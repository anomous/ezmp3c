Quick Start
EZ MP3 Creator is very easy to use with a wizard. Insert an audio cd in your cdrom, and click "Next" button several times, then you can get music files in the output folder (C:\Music by default).

The wizard pages of EZ MP3 Creator guide you to finish your work.

Start: Select the task you want to do and which cdrom device to use (if you have more than one).

Tracks: Select the audio tracks you want to extract and encode, edit tracks' information. The information is to be stored in output files as MP3 ID3 tags, Ogg comments, or WMA attributes.

Options: Configure working parameters.

Progress: Show process progress of extracting and encoding.
